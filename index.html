FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Hannah's Hot Pot

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across Festac Town, Amuwo Odofin, Lekki Phase 1, Victoria Island, and Ikeja. For areas outside these zones, contact us on WhatsApp to confirm availability. A delivery fee applies based on distance.

How do I place an order?

Browse our menu, select your items, and tap "Order Now" to send a WhatsApp message. You can also send "MENU" to +234 816 089 4006 on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through the process.

What are your payment options?

We accept bank transfers, USSD payments, and cash on delivery within Festac. Card payments coming soon. Full payment is required for orders above ₦15,000.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 45 minutes within Festac and Amuwo. For Lekki, VI, and Ikeja, allow 60–90 minutes. Occasion trays and bulk orders require 48-hour advance booking.

Do you cater for events and parties?

Yes! We provide full catering for corporate events, birthdays, weddings, and parties. Minimum 20 persons. 48-hour pre-order required. Contact us on WhatsApp for a customized menu and quote.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We offer diabetic-friendly options (Oat Fufu, Potato Swallow), keto-friendly choices (Cauliflower Pounded Yam), and vegetarian options. Just let us know your requirements when ordering.
